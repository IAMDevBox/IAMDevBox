Why This Matters Now#

Launching a B2B application with robust identity and access management (IAM) is crucial, but deciding on the right billing plan can be overwhelming. With Auth0, you face a critical decision: monthly or annual billing? This choice isn’t just about cost; it directly impacts your development process, financial planning, and overall business strategy. As of January 2024, many startups and established businesses are grappling with this decision, especially after the recent surge in cloud-based services and the need for flexible pricing models.

🚨 Breaking: Recent shifts in cloud service pricing and increased competition highlight the importance of choosing the right billing plan to maximize ROI and minimize risk.

When Should You Choose Monthly Billing?#

Monthly billing is ideal for early-stage projects and rapid iteration cycles. Here’s why:

Launching MVPs and Validating Core Ideas#

When you’re just starting out, your traffic and revenue are unpredictable. You might see a sudden spike in users one month, only to see a drop the next. Monthly billing allows you to adapt to these fluctuations without committing to a long-term financial obligation.

Executing Rapid Iteration Cycles#

Agile development methodologies thrive on flexibility. Monthly billing provides the same agility in your financial planning. You can test new features, gather feedback, and pivot your strategy without the burden of an annual commitment.

Aggressively Testing New Feature Sets#

Sometimes you need access to advanced features for a specific sprint or project. Monthly billing lets you try out these features without locking you into them long-term. It’s like dating before marriage—you can experiment freely.

Handling Unpredictable Traffic#

Unpredictable traffic patterns are common in the early stages of a project. Monthly billing ensures you only pay for what you use, reducing the risk of overspending.

Aligning Your Roadmap#

Your development roadmap is likely to change rapidly. Monthly billing aligns with this fluidity, allowing you to adjust your budget and resources as needed.

Key Takeaways#

  • Ideal for MVPs and early-stage projects.
  • Provides flexibility during rapid iteration cycles.
  • Allows testing of new features without long-term commitment.
  • Manages unpredictable traffic patterns effectively.
  • Aligns with changing development roadmaps.

When Should You Switch to Annual Billing?#

Once you have a baseline of consistent traffic and steady growth, switching to annual billing makes sense. Here’s why:

Optimizing Your Budget#

Annual billing often comes with discounts, effectively giving you a month free. This not only saves money but also reduces administrative overhead.

Reducing Mental Load#

With annual billing, you stop processing invoices 12 times a year. You eliminate the worry of expired credit cards and other administrative tasks. This mental relief allows you to focus on coding and building your product.

Securing Best Possible Rates#

Locking in your rate for a year means you avoid future price increases. This stability is crucial for long-term financial planning.

Scaling SaaS Platforms#

As your platform grows, you need a reliable and predictable billing model. Annual billing provides this stability, enabling you to scale efficiently.

Key Takeaways#

  • Optimizes budget with discounts.
  • Reduces administrative mental load.
  • Secures best possible rates.
  • Supports scaling SaaS platforms.

What Happens When You Upgrade from a Monthly Plan to Annual One?#

One common concern is whether upgrading from a monthly to an annual plan locks you into a fixed rate. Not at all. Auth0 uses proration, which means you only pay the difference between your current plan and the new one. Here’s how it works:

Example Scenario#

Let’s say you start with a monthly Essentials plan costing $100. After three months, you decide to upgrade to a Professional plan, which costs $500 per month. Instead of paying the full annual cost of $6,000 ($500 x 12), you only pay the difference for the remaining nine months:

# Calculate the remaining cost
Current plan cost for 3 months: $100 x 3 = $300
Remaining cost for 9 months at new plan: $500 x 9 = $4,500
Total cost for the year: $300 (initial) + $4,500 (upgrade) = $4,800

This proration ensures you don’t lose out on the unused portion of your current plan.

Key Takeaways#

  • Proration applies when upgrading plans.
  • You only pay the difference between your current and new plan.
  • No wasted money on unused portions.

What Is the Technical Impact of Changing Your Auth0 Billing Cycle?#

Upgrading your billing cycle is purely an administrative task. Here’s what happens technically:

Tenant ID and Domains Remain the Same#

Your Tenant ID and domains stay unchanged. This means no disruptions to your existing configurations.

Environment Variables and API Keys Stay Intact#

All your environment variables and API keys remain untouched. Your application continues to function seamlessly.

No Maintenance Window Required#

You don’t need a maintenance window to switch billing cycles. The upgrade is instant and painless.

Example Transition#

Here’s a step-by-step guide to upgrading your billing cycle:

Log in to your Auth0 Dashboard

Navigate to your Auth0 account and log in.

Go to the Billing Section

Click on the "Billing" tab in the dashboard.

Select Your New Plan

Choose the annual plan that suits your needs.

Review and Confirm

Review the details and confirm the upgrade.

Key Takeaways#

  • Tenant ID and domains remain unchanged.
  • Environment variables and API keys stay intact.
  • No maintenance window required.
  • Instant and seamless upgrade process.

Just Pick One and Ship#

Ultimately, the goal is to spend as little time as possible in the Billing tab. Choose monthly billing for early-stage projects and rapid iterations. Once you have consistent traffic and steady growth, switch to annual billing to save money and reduce administrative overhead.

💜 Pro Tip: Regularly review your usage and adjust your plan as needed to ensure you're getting the best value.

Ready to make the switch? Log in to your Auth0 Dashboard, check your usage, and pick the plan that lets you sleep at night.

If you’re still stuck, just reach out to Auth0 support at customeradvocate@auth0.com for personalized assistance. They can help evaluate your options and ensure you make the right choice.

Conclusion#

Choosing between monthly and annual billing with Auth0 is a critical decision that impacts your financial planning and operational efficiency. By understanding the pros and cons of each option, you can align your billing strategy with your development roadmap and optimize your budget. Whether you’re launching an MVP or scaling a successful platform, the right billing plan can make all the difference.
