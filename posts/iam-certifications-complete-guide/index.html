Following the Ping Identity and ForgeRock merger, the certification ecosystem includes:

Focus: Authentication, Authorization, SSO, Federation

Key Topics:

  • Authentication Trees and Journeys
  • OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect
  • SAML 2.0 federation
  • Policy management
  • Session management

Ideal For: Security engineers, IAM architects, SSO specialists

Key Topics:

  • Managed objects and schema
  • Connectors (LDAP, Database, REST, Scripted)

Frequently Asked Questions#

Which certification should I get first?#

For most people: AM Specialist. Authentication is the foundation of IAM, and AM skills are applicable across all environments.

Are ForgeRock certifications still valid after the Ping merger?#

Yes. ForgeRock certifications remain valid and valuable. The products continue under the ForgeRock brand within Ping Identity.

How often do I need to recertify?#

Most certifications are valid for 2 years. Recertification typically requires passing an updated exam or completing continuing education.

Can I use exam dumps or braindumps?#

No. Exam dumps violate certification agreements and don’t build real skills. Focus on hands-on experience and understanding concepts.
